  MSSG Property management company is in the market working as a mid client between Owners and Renters. As the business is growing, so is the need to manage the company informaton in a more dynamic way. The business realizes that to attain its full potential the company needs to have a robust system to manage its customers.

  The proposed solution to the challenge described above is a web enabled database system for storing and managing metadata related to customers, renters and employees. This application is the proof of concept solution designed by Group#3 of the CSIT 530 class of Fall 2012 at Hood College.
